Our Story

From Local Roots to Regional Scale

Born in Dallas, Texas, CallMe F&L started as a small fulfillment hub serving online sellers. Within two years, we scaled into a global fulfillment network — partnering with leading logistics providers worldwide and integrating with major e-commerce platforms to support businesses across international markets.

Today we process hundreds of orders daily with an accuracy rate that rivals global 3PLs — at prices built for independent sellers and growing brands. We don't just ship packages; we handle the operational complexity so you can focus on what matters: marketing, products, and growth.
